[hShop](https://hshop.erista.me/) is a Homebrew application which allows to installing Homebrew and many games directly from your 3DS. It is like the eShop and is a working alternative to the earlier [FreeShop](https://www.gamebrew.org/wiki/FreeShop_3DS).
### What You Need

* The latest version of [hShop](https://hshop.erista.me/3hs)
+ You can scan [this QR code](https://download2.erista.me/3hs/3hs.cia.png) using the FBI application on your Nintendo 3DS.
* An microSD card with 4GB or more

### Instructions

#### Method 1 - CIA download

Using this method, you will download a CIA file for your 3DS that can be installed to your Nintendo 3DS using the FBI application. Make sure you have [CFW](https://3ds.hacks.guide/) first.

1. Insert your SD card into your computer
1. Create a folder named `cias` on the root of your SD card if it does not already exist
  + The root of the SD card refers to the initial directory on your SD card where you can see the Nintendo 3DS folder, but are not inside of it
1. Copy the CIA file `3hs.cia` from your computer's downloads folder to the `/cias/` of your SD card
1. Reinsert your SD card into your device
1. Power on your device
1. Launch FBI from the home menu.
1. Navigate to `SD` -> `cias`
1. Select "3hs.cia"
1. Select the "Install and delete CIA" option.
1. When the installation is done, press (HOME), then close FBI
You will get a prompt that new software is installed. Then 'open' the new software. You have successfully installed hShop/3HS! You can now install many software, games and much more. For more information, check the [hShop](https://hshop.erista.me/) website.
